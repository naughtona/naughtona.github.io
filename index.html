Welcome

I’m a postgrad student at The University of Melbourne (UoM), where I study Computer Science.

Alongside my studies, I work part-time as a Junior Data Analyst at Brighton Grammar School and serve on both the Education Subcommittee of the Computing and Information Systems Students Association (CISSA) and the General Committee of the University Network for Investing and Trading (UNIT).

In 2018 and 2019, I was lucky to intern at KPMG’s Melbourne Office, working part-time in semester and full-time during University breaks. I worked within the Global Transfer Pricing Services team, where I was able to gain valuable corporate experience in a leading international professional services firm.

Over the past 2 years of studying Computer Science, I have developed several small to medium sized programs, some of which I have made public. Read more about my projects here.

Besides programming, my interests include tennis, snowboarding, surfing, and running. You can read more about me here.
